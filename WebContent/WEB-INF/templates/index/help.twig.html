Contact Us
  • arrow_drop_downWhat is 2CouplesConnect?
    2CouplesConnect is a website and soon Android and IOS app created to help couples find other nearby couples to enjoy in person, double-dates, come over, and do things with. Potential users of our website include the following. You have just moved to a new area and want to make new friends. Or you, or your partner, is pregnant and need to find some new friends who can relate to the life change and would do activities you want to do. Even when you have children and want to find friends you and your partner can hang out with, that won't mind having kids in the house, or even have kids of their own as potential playmates!

    Generally, use 2CouplesConnect when you want to expand/redirect your social circle.

    It's super easy too! Just sign up for an account and enter our minimal information (age, gender, 5 essential questions) and your SO will do the same. Couple up with each other, and complete your couple profile (upload a photo, tell us how long you've been together, if you have kids and your general location). After that, we'll inform you (match) similar nearby couples, so when you are ready -- start chatting and connecting! Answer more questions and spend less time searching by getting better automated recommendations, allowing for more time to chat, and more quickly meet your new couple friends.

  • arrow_drop_downIs 2CouplesConnect free?
    Yes! 2CouplesConnect is completely free sign-up. We do plan to add premium tiered memberships in the future, that will have exclusive benefits.
  • arrow_drop_downWhy do I have to make a separate account from my partner if we are going to be joined anyways?
    Well, we kept in mind that sometimes life happens, and your partner may no longer be your partner later. People break-up or divorce, and maybe later want to use the site. We wanted to make this already hard life change easier to handle on 2CouplesConnect. By having separate profiles, you can un-couple with your partner, and then you could recouple with another person and only have to redo some of your information.
  • arrow_drop_downCan I use 2CouplesConnect if I'm not in a relationship?
    Unfortunately, you can't. We allow you to create an account but after filling out your basic information and the five essential survey questions, we require you to couple up with your partner before you can do anything else. So, if you don't have someone to couple up with, you won't be able to browse other couples or use any features.
  • arrow_drop_downIs 2CouplesConnect available everywhere?
    Yes. All you need is a stable internet connection! Please keep your web browser full screen at 100% zoom for the best visual experience!

  • arrow_drop_downHow do I create a 2CouplesConnect account?
    First, welcome to 2CouplesConnect! We are glad to help you on your quest for the perfect couple friends.

    To sign up for an account just look over to the "create an account" box on our home page and fill out the form (email, first name, last name, password). After that, fill out our basic requirements which will be presented to you as soon as you create a log in and verify your email. With age and gender, just five essential survey questions, you can couple up (your partner will have to do the same steps as outlined). Then fill out your couple profile: upload a photo, let us know about how long you have been dating, if you both have kids or not, what kind of relationship are you both in (dating, married, etc.), allow location, write a "story" for other couples to see and start looking through your recommended couples!

  • arrow_drop_downHow do I edit my profile?
    On your couple page there is a little tab called update my info on the left. Click on that and update any incorrect information or add more information! If you want to update your couple profile, simply click on the tab right next to update profile called "update our info" and do your editing there.
  • arrow_drop_downI want better matches/ I want to change my answer to my survey questions.
    Just go to update survey and answer as many questions as you can! Remember, the more you answer the better your matches will be.
  • arrow_drop_downHow do I search?
    After completing the requirements, a magnify glass will appear on the top right that you can click to define and search for other couples. Don't forget, we also do the hard work for you so if you desire you can just browse recommended couples instead!
  • arrow_drop_downHow do I message another couple?
    To message another couple both you and your partner will have to approve on that couple. Once you both approve you will be allowed to send a message to the other couple. But keep in mind, for receiving couple, both partners need to agree to reply, before they are able to send a response.
  • arrow_drop_downI accidently approved a couple I don't like/ I changed my mind! How do I fix this?
    Don't worry! Its easy. Go to your approved couples and simply change your vote from approve to disapprove. If you accidently declined a couple you like you can just go to declined couples tab and approve them there.
