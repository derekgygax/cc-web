2CouplesConnect
Real Friends, Real Fast
2CouplesConnect is a site to help address the issue of couples going through life stage change events such as -- relocating, getting married, having kids.

Where your existing friends are not the best fit anymore, or just to add to the existing orbit of friends. To find new couple friends -- not his friends, not her friends -- but our friends, that can relate to our life stage.

Not a swingers site, but meant to find couples to hang out with that are on the same page as both of you.
